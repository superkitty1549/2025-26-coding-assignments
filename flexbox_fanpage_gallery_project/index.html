Nightcord at 25:00

Nightcord at 25:00 is one of six singer groups in the rhythm game Project Sekai.

(25時、ナイトコードで。, Nightcord at 25:00) (usually shortened to 25ji or niigo (ニーゴ)) is an underground music circle. The group consists of composer and leader Yoisaki Kanade, lyricist Asahina Mafuyu, illustrator Shinonome Ena, and music video editor Akiyama Mizuki.

This music group is most associated in the game with an area that has a lake with stage truss, pillars and prisms inside it and rectangular prisms decorate the floor. It is unknown how far the lake goes on for. Around the edge of the lake is an arch and a platform, where a single sprout grows, and floating paving stones that lead to a door, which cannot be opened. With this, a new stage was created. The area is a rectangular room with irregular window framework on the walls and a star-like light fixture from the ceiling.
